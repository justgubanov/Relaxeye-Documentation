Frequently Asked Questions

How does this app work and is it effective?

▶
The app follows the Bates Method, guiding eye movement with an animated dot for relaxation.

How often should I do eye exercises per day?

▶
Most users benefit from 1-2 sessions per day, especially during long periods of screen time.

What are common use cases for this app?

▶
Great for anyone using screens for extended periods, or wanting to reduce eye strain.

What platforms is this app available on?

▶
Currently available on iPhone and iPad only.

How do I synchronize progress across devices?

▶
In the app, go to Settings -> General, and toggle "Share across devices". Make sure you're logged into your Apple account.

Does the app collect any data?

▶
No data is collected. Privacy Policy

Is this app a medical treatment?

▶
No, please consult a doctor for medical advice.
